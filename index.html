Growing up watching documentaries narrated by the great Sir David Attenborough, I developed love and passion for the natural world around me. Even in the urban landscape of Singapore, we can still find pockets of biodiversity in the greenery dotting our little red dot.

This site showcases the wonders of Singapore's biodiversity through the lens of a nature lover, and seeks to inspire the love for Singapore's unique forms of life and a passion to save the planet we live in.
